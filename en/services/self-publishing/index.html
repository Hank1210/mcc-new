Publishing Routes

Which Path Suits You?

We explain the options and help with the decision.

Traditional Publisher

The publisher handles editing, cover design, marketing and distribution. Authors give up rights and receive only a share of revenues.

Literary Agent

Agents pitch manuscripts to publishers, increasing acceptance rates. Compensation is commission-based.

Wholesale / Bookshops

Books sold through wholesalers to bookshops. Requires stable sales volume.

Amazon Seller Central

Direct marketplace sales. The author handles production, storage and shipping.

Amazon Advantage

Commission-based programme: Amazon purchases and distributes printed books.

Recommended

Self-Publishing via KDP

100% creative freedom over cover, content and price. Up to 70% royalties on eBooks. The most direct path to your own book.
